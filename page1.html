What is Key Club?

We live through service!

Key Club International is the BIGGEST high school community-service club that supports charitable events and causes, allowing students to participate as volunteers. The club provides opportunities to perform service build character and develop leadership through the vehicle of service.

Our Pledge

I pledge, on my honor, to uphold the Objects of Key Club International; to build my home, school and community; to serve my nation and God; and combat all forces which tend to undermine these institutions.

Core Values

Our club revolves around these core values
and aim to build upon individuals.

Leadership

"The quality of a leader is reflected in the standards
they set for themselves. "

Caring      

"Without a sense of caring, there can be
no sense of community."

Character

Character is built by everyday acts of kindess, integrity
and teamwork to grow stronger relationships and communities.

Inclusiveness

We aim to include every member so that
nobody feels left out.
