What is Key Club?

Key Club International is the BIGGEST high school community-service club that supports charitable events and causes, allowing students to participate as volunteers. The club provides opportunities to perform service build character and develop leadership through the vehicle of service.

Learn More

Our Pledge

I pledge, on my honor, to uphold the Objects of Key Club International; to build my home, school and community; to serve my nation and God; and combat all forces which tend to undermine these institutions.
